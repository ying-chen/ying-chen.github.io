Unfogging the Future

你说这有啥好不明白的。


Mr. Course Director came to speak on research colloquium and we met for an apfelschorle by seerhein. He used to be my referee, but we never met for an onsite talk – neither before nor after the recommendation was sent. Therefore, it was the time to show up and to thank him for his “very positive” reference – since I am the only one that have no access to this letter, I am the last one to realize its power, from the committee’s comments.

I told him it perhaps was the best reference I got so far. But he said I deserved it, due to my performance. I asked him usually what will be written in a reference. He provided two sentences. One, “he is the second best student I have ever met in my academic life”, and two, “I would recommend this student to pursue a position in any top ten academic intuitions around UK except Oxcambridge”. “It also depends on who write it”, he said, “then the power comes”.

每个席上总归有那么几个逻辑严密，话术稳健的朋友。They determine to stay in academia after graduation. They asked about Mr. Head, quite strategically. To be frank, since he is still in position, “in one piece”, it must be better to ask himself, rather than circulating rumours as if YOU were the witness. 有些人的回答，或是着了问话人的道，或是破其意而故意为之，无论哪种，非蠢即坏。

It seems that we do have distinct opinions on what consists of political correctness. It is common that our statement is politically correct in a certain community while it offends others. 做研究在任何时间任何地点都不是一件拥有绝对自由的事情 – this is a professional common knowledge, among us.

又想起一个二月份的闲聊。“这是城市化的必经过程，人家不是没有，人家是100年前就完成了。我们在这里享受的是100年前的成果。”“那凭什么我就应该回去承受这种阵痛呢。”

一样，道理是没错，不过听了难受。
